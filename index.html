Who we are

About INFINITY

Professional installation and support for legal GPS tracking solutions in Egypt.

Infinity specializes in installing and maintaining GPS tracking systems provided by ETIT (Egyptian company for Tracking and Information Technology).

We were established in 2016, right after ETIT was authorized by the National Telecommunication Regulatory Authority (NTRA) as the Egyptian legal entity for GPS tracking nationwide.
