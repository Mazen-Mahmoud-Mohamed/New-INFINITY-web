About Company

Infinity is a professional company specialized in providing the service of installation for GPS tracking system provided by ETIT (Egyptian company for Tracking and Information Technology).

Infinity was established on 2016 immediately after the authorization of ETIT to be Egyptian legal entity to provide GPS tracking system in Egypt by National Telecommunication Regulatory Authority (NTRA).

Our Services

Legal GPS Tracking Solutions Powered BY ETIT

As a fleet manager, it is crucial to your business to not only track your vehicles, but to have access to immediate, secure information about your fleet at all times. You need to monitor the locations and statuses of their vehicles and drivers, without incurring the complexity of a full fleet management system.

Fleet management solutions by ETIT present an effective offering which suits fleets of varying sizes and types - from trucks to light commercial vehicles, to motorcycles. Even the entry-level fleet management ETIT portal enables customers to always have direct control over their mobile assets and workforce.
