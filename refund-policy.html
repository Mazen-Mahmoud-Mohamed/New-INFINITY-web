INFINITY Total-Com Solutions aims to deliver reliable GPS, fleet, and security products. This policy outlines when refunds may apply and how to request support for your order.

Eligibility

Refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Items that have been installed, activated, or customized may not qualify for a full refund. Please contact us within a reasonable period after delivery if you believe your order requires review.

Refund Process

To initiate a refund request, contact our team with your order details. Approved refunds are processed using the original payment method when possible. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
