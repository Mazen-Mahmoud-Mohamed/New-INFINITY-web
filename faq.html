Ordering

How do I place an order? Browse our products page, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will need an account to complete payment.

What payment methods are accepted? We accept card payments through our secure checkout. Available methods are shown on the payment page.

Installation

Is installation included? Installation is listed separately on applicable products. Our team coordinates scheduling after your order is confirmed.

Where do you install? We serve customers across Egypt. See our shipping & delivery policy for more details.

Support

How can I reach your team? Call +20 10 21111480, email Mohamed.Zidan@infinity-egy.net, or use the contact section on our home page.

Account

Do I need an account? Yes, an account is required to checkout and track orders. You can sign in or create one on the auth page.

Where can I view my orders? Signed-in customers can view order history from My Orders.
